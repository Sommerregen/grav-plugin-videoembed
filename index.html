About

VideoEmbed - plugin for Grav CMS, that convert links to popular video sharing services into embed video elements. Plugin supports responsiveness video size(16:9) and more video sharing services:

  1. Youtube
  2. Vimeo
  3. Coub.com
  4. Vine.co
  5. .. you can propose more services here
